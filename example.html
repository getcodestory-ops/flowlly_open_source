1.0 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

In the month of May, interior finishes throughout the building continued as did work along the building envelope, notably related to the EIFS scope.

Interior boarding on level 21 and millwork on levels 16 and 17 was delayed, requiring recovery strategies to be implemented to mitigate the risk to the project.

Commencement of installation of the elevators was further delayed in May; but is expected to proceed in early June. A firm completion date has been provided to and acknowledged by the elevator subtrade, with strategies required to meet the date only able to be evaluated once work on site progresses.

Interim occupancy and substantial completion dates are impacted by one-and-a-half (1.5) and two (2) months, respectively due to mechanical and electrical commissioning scheduled start dates. Meetings with the third-party commissioning agent and necessary subtrades continued to in the month of May, a positive step for the project.

Bockstael continues to work with the BIPV subtrade and Consultants to navigate through issues associated with completion and submission of shop drawings. Progress was made in the month of May, and final shop drawings are expected to be complete in June.

The cumulative SPI for the project is 0.91, indicating the work is behind schedule.

The contract value as of May 31, 2025, is $62,719,217.08 and the monthly progress claim amounted to $2,319,261.19.

There was one (1) safety incident in this reporting period.
